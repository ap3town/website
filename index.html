AP3.TOWN

AP3 is a decentralized & deflationary currency on Binance Smart Chain that rewards it's holders, buyers and LP farmers. It is designed in such a way that the appropriate tax for each type of transaction is immediately reflected across all token holder balances, resulting in a steady, frictionless yield. Holders and LP farmers receive a portion of every transaction that occurs, and even more if it is sell-side transaction. Additionaly, 1% of each sale TX is sent to the AP3 Vault, which redistributes the deposited funds to potential buyers.

To begin earning rewards, simply purchase tokens and keep them in your wallet. All rewards are delivered directly to your AP3 address after each TX.

The banana on the cake is the gorilla mode, which is still under wraps but will be unveiled soon.

The circulating supply provides all rewards. This eliminates the need to mint or add new tokens to circulation. Instead, the token's value will rise over time, and the token supply will end up in the hands of the alpha apes - strong AP3 holders.

HOW TO BUY AP3 TOKEN

A SHORT GUIDE TO PURCHASING AP3 TOKEN WITH BINANCE COIN

INSTALL METAMASK WALLET

Metamask is a browser extension & mobile app that acts as a wallet used to store BEP-20 (BSC) such as AP3.

Follow the instructions from Metamask on how to install the wallet in your browser or device.

ADD BSC NETWORK

You will need to add the Binance Smart Chain Network (BSC) to be able to interact with AP3.TOWN website

Firstly, select the button on the top labeled "Ethereum Mainnet" in your Metamask wallet and click "Custom RPC" and Enter the Details below:

Network Name: Binance Smart Chain
New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
ChainID: 56
Symbol: BNB
Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

For a more detailed guide visit the official guide from Binance.


CREATE A BINANCE ACCOUNT TO PURCHASE BINANCE COIN (BNB)

You will need to create a Binance Account to purchase Binance Coin (BNB). Alternatively, you can also look into using Binance Bridge if we dont' with to signup

To create a Binance account go to Binance.com and create an account or log into you Binance account if you already have one.

EXCHANGE / BUY BNB

Exchange BTC, ETH or any other supported token for BNB on the binance exchange. You can also directly buy BNB on the Exchange with Fiat.

WITHDRAW BNB TO METAMASK

In your Binance account, open your BNB wallet then tap on Withdraw.
Select Binance Smart Chain (BSC) as the Transfer Network.

Ensure that you select that you want to withdraw BEP-20 (BSC) tokens.

Copy your address from your Metamask wallet and choose the amount you want to withdraw

Make sure the network tab in your Metamask wallet is set to "Binance Smart Chain"

Complete the steps to withdraw.
Wait for the exchange to process your request. Once it is confirmed, you will immediately receive BNB to your Smart Chain address.


ADD AP3 TO YOUR WALLET

Make sure the network tab in your Metamask wallet is set to "Binance Smart Chain"
In your Metamask wallet scroll down to the button "Add Token"
Select "Custom Token"
Paste the AP3 contract address into the "Token Contract Address" field. All other fields will automatically be filled in.

0x000000000000000000000aP3

In your metamask wallet scroll down to the button "Add Token"
Click "Next" then click "Add Token"
Congratulations your now a AP3 TOKEN holder!


EXCHANGE BNB FOR AP3 ON PANCAKESWAP

Make sure the network tab in your Metamask wallet is set to "Binance Smart Chain"Follow the PancakeSwap link to exchange on website and swap your BNB for AP3Make sure slippage is set to 5% (sell 9%) by selecting the gear icon next to the "Exchange" title.

Ensure when swapping BNB for AP3 the Official contract address is selected for the "to" token.
